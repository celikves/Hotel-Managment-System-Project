HOTEL MAZARIN


JW Marriott Los Angeles Mazarin sets a new standard for luxury. Showcasing unbeatable placement
in the heart of downtown, only steps away from the excitement of Mazarin, the Convention Center,
Microsoft Theatre and STAPLES Center, our hotel is ideally suited for both
business and leisure travelers to Los Angeles. Our hotel rooms and suites feature luxury bedding,
a flat-screen TV, an ergonomic workspace and a marble bathroom; many rooms offer magnificent downtown views.
Savor fresh local fare at our on-site restaurants, pamper yourself at our spa or relax by our sparkling
outdoor pool. Luxury abounds at JW Marriott Los Angeles Mazarin.
